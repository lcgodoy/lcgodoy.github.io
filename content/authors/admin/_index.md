Hi, my name is Lucas. I'm from Brazil and currently, I'm a P.h.D. Candidate in
Statistics at UConn. 

I have chosen statistics when I was 18 years old, at that time I was working on
the 2010 Brazilian Census (at
[IBGE](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_Institute_of_Geography_and_Statistics))
as a supervisor. I was amazed about several things, more importantly, how complex
and interesting was the process of collecting high-quality data. When I realized
that the statisticians were the ones in charge of the planning of such an
intricate operation, I made some research about the profession. Again, I was
astonished. This time it was not for the complexity of the tasks themselves, but
for how versatile statistics is, how helpful it is for so many (if not all)
fields of study (and for society in general).
